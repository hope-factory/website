THE 7 ESSENTIALS
 FOR HEALTHY GROWTH

1. IMPACT OPTIMIZATION

You can't know if you're getting somewhere if you don't know where you're going. True growth happens in a context of tuning toward your desired outcomes, indicators, and goals. 

2. LEAN DESIGN INNOVATION

Identifying and refining your recipe for growth best happens when you empathize with those you serve, understand the problem, prototype potential solutions, and experiment! 

3. GROWTH HACKING PROFICIENCY

A relentless pursuit of growth means testing, analyzing, problem-solving, and testing some more. If you want to grow, you must get creative and analyze potential paths forward.

4. FUNDRAISING/SALES MOMENTUM

Designing healthy processes of revenue allows for sustainable growth over the longterm. Once the flywheel starts going, you start growing. Innovation and experimentation are key.

5. GROWTH-EMPOWERING LEADERSHIP

A growth culture comes from the top and extends to everyone in the organization. Leaders must be dedicated, humble, collaborative, empowering and focused on growing impact.

6. EFFECTIVE STORYTELLING

If you want others to go with you somewhere great, become great at telling stories. Nothing moves people like a good story well told. Make your story good. Tell your story well.

7. TECHNOLOGY ACCELERATION

You can accelerate the growth of your good through the effective use of technology. Your tech solutions better not be slowing you down; they should drive you forward.
