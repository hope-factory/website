7 ESSENTIALS FOR 
HEALTHY GROWTH

Empower yourself to do more good.

Our research and experience proves success is possible when you focus on these 7 key ingredients to growth. 

1. Impact Optimization
With a clearly defined set of outcomes you are seeking to achieve, you can measure the impact you are having and track the indicators along the way. This allows you to grow in the right direction as you determine which activities you implement move you forward.

2. Lean Design Innovation
Learning how to learn is one of the most basic building blocks of growth. For stuck organizations, growth requires innovation, and innovation is successfully achieved by embracing the iterative process of a human-centered, design thinking approach, regularly creating simple prototypes and experimenting.

3. Growth Hacking Proficiency 
By embracing a startup's mentality focusing relentlessly on growth, any organization can develop their "secret sauce" for growth by rigorously implementing analytics and creatively testing different tactics to see what works for them and their audience.

4. Fundraising/Sales Momentum 
Once you find your spark, consistent growth is achieved by implementing sllid systems of growth that allow true momentum to build as the flywheel turns. Experimentation leads to insight, and you capitalize on that insight with momentum-building implementation.

5. Growth-Empowering Leadership 
One of the most important contributing factors to growth for an organization is a growth culture, and all culture comes from the top. If the leadership values growth, invests in growth, and collaboratively, humbly, diligently empowers the team to grow, amazing things can happen.

6. Effective Storytelling 
We want to see growth that changes the world for the better. We are on a journey for true impact, and we want people to come along so lives are changed. We must live compelling stories and become experts at crafting and relaying those narratives.

7. Technology Acceleration
As James Collins pointed out, rarely does technology drive growth, but regularly can technology accelerate that growth. Too many stuck organizations aren't investing in the tools that would help them fly and are content to keep anchors that drag them down. Your technology solutions must reinforce your key growth factors.
