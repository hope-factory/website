NO DONOR LEFT BEHIND

Developing Lifetime Donors

When it comes to non-profits and fundraising it is universally accepted that “people give to people, people they know and people they trust.” Anyone raising money will tell you asking for money is “relational.” How is it then that the typical non-profit will see 55% of those who gave one year not give the following year?

The relational model is not working or better stated, it’s not practiced. Many are unaware their back doors are open and donors with their dollars are leaving in droves. This is the #1 failure of non-profits today and for many non-profits it means failing to receive thousands, hundreds of thousands, or even millions of dollars in gifts annually! Donors are being left behind.

During his 35+ years in consulting, Dr. Bill Hautt has seen this play out in scores of organizations across the US and Canada and believes it’s time to put a spotlight on this issue. His new book, No Donor Left Behind, is scheduled to be released in the Spring of 2020.

Here are some of his chapter titles:

  • Is Donor Attrition a “Zero Sum” Game? Maybe.

  • Building Donor Communities

  • “We’re a Start-Up. We Don’t Have Donors to Leave Behind”
