MAJOR DONOR PROCESS MANAGEMENT

Get empowered to grow by unburdening yourself from the pain and stress of managing your major donor processes.

If you feel stuck without the support or systems needed to engage donors well, offload the practical process management of major donor fundraising for a fraction of the cost of hiring additional development staff.

WHAT WE DO:

1. Craft Your Case
You have an amazing story, let's get it out there! We'll create a case statement that tells your story in a beautiful and compelling way so that it appeals to donors and makes conversations around funding easy.

2. Empower Your Leadership
People give to people who ask them. You already have great people arond you. We will empower your staff and volunteer leadership so you have the team you need to succeed.

3. Classify Your Prospects 
You have a wealth of donors currently sitting in your donor file, and whatâ€™s next is to qualify and cultivate them. We'll review your donor file to organize and classify your prospects.

4. Develop Your Strategy
Your major donor processes need to fit into a comprehensive fundraising strategy and environment that is feasible, relational, and well managed. We will help assess your donor tiers and overall strategy, creating a path forward to success.

5. Create Your Plan
You need an individualized plan for each donor and donor segment to ensure you are properly cultivating, asking, and stewarding your donors. Both you and your donors will then have your needs met in the context of a strong relationship.

6. Manage the Implementation
Because we provide practical implementation of the plan, you can focus on relationships with donors and the mission that got you into this work. We'll provide process management, accountability, database support, and regular reviews.

7. Celebrate Your Success
You want to eradicate the pain and stress of fundraising, and one of the most critical steps is taking the time to celebrate your successes... and to do it alongside your donors! Together you are changing the world. Take the time to enjoy it!
